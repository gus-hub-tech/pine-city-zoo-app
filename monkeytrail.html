MONKEY TRAIL

Welcome to the Monkey Trail, where you can discover the fascinating world of monkeys!

Our Monkey Trail is home to a variety of species, including capuchins, macaques, and tamarins. You can observe these incredible creatures up close and learn about their unique behaviors and habitats.

With interactive displays and informative signage, the Monkey Trail is a great place for visitors of all ages to explore the wonders of the primate kingdom.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from the main pathways.
