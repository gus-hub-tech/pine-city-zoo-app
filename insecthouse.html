INSECT-HOUSE

Welcome to the Insect House, where you can discover the fascinating world of insects!

Our Insect House is home to a variety of species, including butterflies, beetles, and ants. You can observe these incredible creatures up close and learn about their unique behaviors and habitats.

With interactive displays and informative signage, the Insect House is a great place for visitors of all ages to explore the wonders of the insect kingdom.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Insect House is located near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from the main pathways.
