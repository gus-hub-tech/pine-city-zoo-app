AMPHITHEATER

Learn and be entertained. Come and catch daily educational and entertaining shows, talks and demonstrations at the amphitheatre.

The amphitheater is a large, open-air venue designed for performances and events. It features tiered seating that provides excellent views of the stage from all angles. The amphitheater is surrounded by lush gardens and is a popular spot for concerts, theater productions, and community gatherings.

With its natural acoustics and beautiful setting, the amphitheater is a favorite among both performers and audiences. It can accommodate a large number of spectators and is equipped with state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find the amphitheater located in the central garden area, easily accessible from all parts of the zoo.
